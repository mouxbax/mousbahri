About

Mous Bahri is a Lebanese indie queer contemporary singer, born in Beirut, based in Paris. All-round artist: he writes, composes, produces his songs, and creates and performs his own shows. His music bridges both shores of the Mediterranean, between Oriental, Pop and Avant-garde Electronic. A genre of his own.

Member of the agency of artists in exile, Paris. Releases via Coast Cove Records.
